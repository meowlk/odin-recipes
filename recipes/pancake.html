Pancakes

Description

This is a bulk whole wheat pancake mix recipe that my family loves, and it is very easy to make! It also makes good waffles.

Making this always brings back memories of those good ol' times..

Ingredients

  • 5 cups whole wheat flour
  • 3 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 2 ½ tablespoons baking powder
  • 4 teaspoons baking soda
  • 4 teaspoons salt

Steps

  1. Whisk whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl; store in an airtight container.
  2. Mix 1 cup of pancake mix, 1 cup of milk, 1 egg, and 2 tablespoons of oil together in a large bowl. Cook pancakes on a hot griddle and serve with your choice of toppings.
